# Request a Custom Offer: How to Get Exactly What You Want in Nyali

When you plan a Kenyan Coast trip or an extended Nyali stay, you are not just booking a place to sleep. You may be designing a family reunion in a [large Nyali villa](/blog/luxury-8-9-bedroom-villas-nyali-large-groups-family-reunions), a birthday surprise by the pool, a dietary-specific chef dinner, or an itinerary that combines transport, errands, and a private experience.

The problem is that travel catalogs are rigid. Fixed packages rarely match real group travel, special timing, strict menus, baby supplies, or multi-stop plans. The old solution was messy WhatsApp coordination: repeating dates to different vendors, chasing prices, and hoping every detail stayed remembered.

Tembea Bila Matata's [Request a Custom Service](/request-custom-service) and experience-specific **Request a Custom Offer** flow gives you a cleaner way to ask for exactly what you need.

![Tembea Bila Matata My Bookings dashboard showing a pending custom offer](/blog-images/custom-offer-my-bookings-dashboard.svg "After you submit a custom request, it stays visible in My Bookings with the amount due, status, brief, and next step.")

## Why Fixed Travel Catalogs Fall Short

Standard booking forms work well for simple plans. They struggle when your trip needs flexibility.

You might want an early airport pickup from Moi International Airport, a gluten-free Swahili-fusion dinner, a villa stocked before check-in, infant formula from a specific shop, or a custom Diani day with snorkeling, lunch, and a sunset stop.

When your plan does not fit a checkbox, you should not have to compromise. A custom offer turns the idea into a structured booking record with clear dates, guest count, location, budget, payment status, and messages.

## How the Custom Offer Flow Works

**1. Submit your brief.** Use [Request a Custom Service](/request-custom-service) for broad logistics, or choose **Request a Custom Offer** on eligible experiences like the [Mambrui Sand Dunes Adventure](/book/experience/d30f73f0-c34c-463d-a9eb-6d18a866a3ca). Add dates, guest count, route ideas, shopping notes, menu needs, budget, location, and reference images.

**2. Pay the $5 creditable request fee.** The fee confirms serious intent and is credited toward the final approved offer. In KES, the checkout shows the equivalent amount, such as KSh650 depending on the app rate.

**3. Receive a tailored proposal.** The admin team or local host reviews the brief and returns a fixed-price offer with a note explaining what is included.

**4. Decide from My Bookings.** Open [My Bookings](/bookings), review the offer, accept and pay the balance, request changes through messages, or decline if it no longer fits.

## Smart Ways to Use Custom Requests in Nyali

**Pre-arrival villa welcome:** Combine [airport pickup](/services/drive) with [grocery delivery](/book/errand/4023b774-4a71-4e6a-bec3-ece4a2850f21), fresh snacks, drinks, toiletries, and breakfast basics before you arrive.

**Curated Diani day:** Request private transport from Nyali, snorkeling or reef time, lunch at [Ali Barbour's Cave Restaurant](https://alibarbours.com/), and a sunset stop near Kongo River.

**Birthday or anniversary setup:** Plan a cake, flowers, photographer, private chef, poolside decor, or a dhow-style sundowner through one brief.

**Old Town and culture day:** Bundle a guided walk around [Fort Jesus](https://museums.co.ke/fort-jesus-mombasa), spice-market stops, lunch, and return transport to your Nyali stay.

**Remote work or corporate logistics:** Arrange recurring driver routes, meeting pickups, [laundry and ironing](/book/errand/18a17f24-609d-4c1a-81ba-db843cc3c29b), and group meal support.

**Family care:** Pair [in-villa babysitting](/book/errand/8c856877-039d-467e-805e-c101d43ed93b) with child-friendly meal prep, errands, or a gentle evening plan for parents.
